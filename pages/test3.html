🧠 Core Concepts

The Bloch sphere represents the state of a qubit as a point on a unit sphere. Every pure state |ψ⟩ is a point on the surface:

|ψ⟩ = cos(θ/2) |0⟩ + e^{iφ} sin(θ/2) |1⟩

State components in 3D:

x = sinθ cosφ
y = sinθ sinφ
z = cosθ
  • |0⟩ is the North Pole (θ = 0)
  • |1⟩ is the South Pole (θ = π)
  • Equator states are superpositions
